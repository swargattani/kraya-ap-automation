FAQs

Frequently asked questions.

Answers about features, integrations, and support. Reach out if you don't see what you need.

What is Kraya?

Kraya is an end-to-end procurement platform designed to streamline sourcing, enhance supplier management, and optimize the procurement process with advanced automation and real-time analytics.

How does Kraya simplify procurement for companies?

Automated workflows, real-time spend analysis, and integrated communication tools make it easier for companies to manage suppliers, control costs, and streamline procurement.

What benefits do vendors receive from Kraya?

Access to tenders, streamlined onboarding, free quotation management, and analytics designed to improve visibility and facilitate easier interactions with companies.

Can Kraya integrate with existing ERP systems?

Yes — Kraya supports integration with major ERP systems, ensuring seamless connectivity and data flow between your existing infrastructure and our platform.

Is Kraya secure?

End-to-end encryption and robust security measures protect your data and ensure compliance — a secure environment for all procurement activities.
